◊ This website is a gallery of Hattrick logos I've made since spring 2004.

◊ Hattrick is a sophisticated on-line football game where you have to manage your own club to it's way of international success. It's based in Sweden and is running since 1997 having now grown to the level of more than 500.000 active users from all around the world and is still growing fast.

◊ Thanks to the grown-up and always creative Hattrick players community many aspects in Hattrick have developed their own local culture around them. So has it been with Hattrick team logos that many artists from anywhere in the world are creating to add more individuality to each Hattrick team as well as to challenge other logo makers and the skills of one's own.

◊ Making HT logos is some kind of a sport and it's full of personal pleasure, hence I have been doing most of these logos for free. However a payed logo request has and will always be treated as a higher priority task to me.

◊ As for myself, I'm a professional 21 years old computer graphics artist working mostly with prints for adversting, logos, corporate identities as well as web graphics and 3D graphics.
